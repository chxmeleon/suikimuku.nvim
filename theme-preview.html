Theme Philosophy

翠 (Sui) - Atmospheric Teal Garden

Multi-palette fusion: your design colors + Makoto Shinkai atmosphere. Ethereal teal, emerald flow, ocean breeze creating cinematic depth with aqua-mint dreams.

輝 (Ki) - Crystal Radiant Light

Pure radiant light concept - cool, crisp, luminous. Morning light through crystal with minimal warmth. Ocean-breeze + crystal-waves palette fusion.

無 (Mu) - WCAG AAA Grayscale

Maximum contrast (21:1 ratio) pure monochrome. WCAG AAA compliant for accessibility. Eliminates all color distractions for deep focus.

空 (Ku) - Terminal Solarized

Perfect harmony with your kitty terminal. Deep ocean tones with golden accents. Seamless workflow between terminal and Neovim.
